SPONSORSHIP PROSPECTUS

Why Sponsor PyCon Balkan 2019?

As a sponsor you get a unique chance to showcase your product or service as well as recruit top talent in Balkan region. PyCon Balkan 2019 is Balkan’s largest gathering of Python developers. Conference draws a diverse crowd with backgrounds in many different industries allowing you to engage new demographics as well as chance to support this amazing growing community. We are the first.

Core Facts

  • PyCon Balkan is an annual conference, organized by Python Belgrade Organization, that takes place in the Balkan region each year since 2018. For an Idea of what to expect, take a look at previous year info.
  • The key aim of PyCon Balkan is to connect the region and provide a real-time meeting opportunity for developers and companies from the region and internationally.
  • We thrive on helping the growth of the diverse Python community in the Balkans, promoting Python programming language, and becoming a place where you can learn new things and improve yourself professionally.

What to expect this year:

  • Full 3 day conference consisting of talks, tutorials, workshops and other educational and networking activities
  • Over 30 speakers consisting of leading regional and international experts in the Python programming language and the IT industry
  • Expected 500 attendees from the region and internationally
  • Special additional recruiting opportunities for major sponsors
  • Social and networking events
  • Working language of the conference will be English
  • 1000m2 of exclusive conference space in Hilton Hotel, Belgrade
